## Why Attesta?

Traditional cryptocurrency wallets have a fundamental UX problem: they require users to manage long seed phrases that are:
- Easy to lose or forget
- Vulnerable to phishing attacks
- Difficult to recover
- Poor user experience

Attesta solves this by using the same technology that powers modern authentication - **WebAuthn and passkeys**. The same technology that lets you unlock your phone with your face or fingerprint.
